Who We Are

Khukha Youth Welfare Trust is a dedicated, non-profit organization that was actively mobilized and formed with a noble goal in 2013: to seamlessly empower the youth and provide essential, life-saving support to underprivileged areas. Over the past decade, we have grown from a small neighborhood initiative into a highly reliable and widespread pillar of hope for countless struggling families.

We firmly believe that our youth are not just the leaders of tomorrow; they are the active changemakers of today. By heavily investing in their education, health, moral training, and personal development, we are building a stronger, more united, and resilient foundation for our entire society.

Our devoted volunteers work passionately day and night, demonstrating profound empathy, true compassion, and exemplary leadership to those who need it most. Whether it is responding to natural disasters, providing financial aid for school fees, or running free medical clinics, our team stands on the front lines. Through unity and relentless hard work, we aim to eradicate inequality and foster a peaceful, self-sustaining society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to proactively alleviate poverty, promote access to quality education, and ensure basic healthcare services for the vulnerable segments of society. We strive to create sustainable welfare programs that empower individuals to become self-reliant.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every young person is educated, healthy, and equipped with the necessary skills to lead a successful life. We dream of a society built on equality, compassion, and shared prosperity, free from discrimination and hardship.

Our Focus Areas

Educational Support

We provide school supplies, tuition fee assistance, and career counseling to students so that financial constraints do not hinder their educational journeys.

Health Camps

Frequent medical camps, blood donation drives, and awareness seminars are organized to tackle health emergencies and promote well-being.

Poverty Alleviation

We deploy ration distribution campaigns during hard times and help deserving youth find small local employment opportunities.
